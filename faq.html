What is Hy U?

Hy U is a networking app that allows users to connect with potential matches based on their location and shared interests. Users can view profiles, swipe right to like or left to pass, and if two users mutually like each other, they can start chatting.

How does Hy U work?

Hy U uses a searching system to present users with potential matches. When you see a profile, you can swipe right to indicate interest or swipe left to pass. If both users swipe right on each other, it's a match and they can start chatting.

How do I edit my profile?

You can edit your profile by tapping on your profile icon, then selecting the "Edit Info" option. Here, you can update your bio, photos, and other details.

Can I message anyone I match with?

Yes, once you've matched with someone, you can start messaging them within the Hy U app.

How do I report or block someone?

You can report and block users by going to their profile, tapping the three dots in the upper right corner, and selecting the appropriate option.

Hy U has safety features like photo verification and integration with Noonlight (a personal safety app). However, it's important to exercise caution and follow safety tips when meeting people from online platforms.

How do I delete my Hy U account?

You can delete your account by going to the settings, scrolling down, and selecting "Delete Account."

Can I see who liked me on Hy U?

Hy U Gold subscribers have the ability to see a list of users who have liked their profile before they start swiping.

How do I improve my chances of getting matches?

Having a complete and interesting profile with clear photos, a catchy bio, and shared interests can improve your chances of getting matches. Being respectful and engaging in genuine conversations also helps.

What is the search Surge feature?

Search Surge is a feature that notifies you when there's a higher-than-usual activity of users swiping in your area. It's designed to increase your chances of matching during peak usage times.

Can I unmatch someone?

Yes, you can unmatch with someone by going to the chat, tapping on the user's name at the top, and selecting the "Unmatch" option.

How does Hy U's algorithm work?

Hy U's algorithm takes into account various factors like your location, swiping behavior, profile information, and more to determine which profiles are shown to you and how your profile is shown to others.
